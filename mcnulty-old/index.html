Writing Prompt: Seek Those That Ride

Growing up I lived in Washington, D.C. in the heyday of WMATA's metro. The public transit system has suffered greatly in recent years but while I was little I would travel all over the district with my elderly neighbor, Anne. She would take me to the Zoo or my favorite Italian delicatessen, which I make sure to visit every time I return home. Very often did Anne and I find ourselves on a red tiled platform under one of Harry Weese's distinctive arches. The center platform stations were my favorite.

Adventures in Turkish Propaganda

Sometime last year the Turkish Cyber group Ayyıldız Tim took advantage of a vulnerability in my hosting provider and wiped my site from the servers, replacing it with some untranslatable animated single page website that rotated through glamour shots of soldiers and heart wrenching pictures of little girls crying. I was a little peeved at all this but for the most part it was my own fault for not personally checking the integrity of the systems I used. I didn't really consider the site important enough to back up as I had copies of the framework, but I've put it back up now and want to fill it out a little more.
