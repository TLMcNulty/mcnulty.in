Services

Gemini is a small video and photo house serving the Boston area.

Production

We can take charge of your idea or help complete it from nebulous idea to video on screen. We excel at drone video, product and location features, and events. All work is deliverable in a range or formats. 4K native.

Edit

Don't want to pay for a full crew from start to finish? Don't need to? Provide us the footage and as little or as much direction as you'd like and we'll export you something you love.

Consulting

Need us for anything in between? Hire us for external direction, production, or editing advice. Invite us to a brainstorming session, we'll weigh in on stylistic choices and story direction, or technical aspects like composition and lighting.

RIT Ambulance

National Collegiate EMS Foundation Video of the Year submission.

Harrison and I were both volunteer EMTs while at RIT, and one year we decided to go up for the video contest our national foundation organized. We spent a lot of time and care on this video and unfortunetly lost. Feedback from the judges was that it was "too sleepy." Seems our professional attempts didn't live up to the action and thrill the judges wanted from an emergency services showcase, though we explicitly decided to not go that route.

When Life Gives You Lemons

MIT EMS

Extrication Drill Feature

Given our background in EMS it's not unreasonable that we're now friendly with MIT's collegiate EMS program. After a fraternity rush car bash, MIT EMS runs an extrication evolution for training purposes. Tom shot and edited a small smash reel for them, as well as a ~40 minute overview of the scenario as a breakdown for futher training and review. It was our hope this video would win the NCEMSF Video of the Year contest that we were denied so many years ago, but unfortunetly MIT was not able to submit for 2018.

  • Date: Mid 2017
  • Client: MIT EMS
  • Category: Video Production
